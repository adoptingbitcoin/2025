At Adopting Bitcoin 2025, we’re embracing the Network Effect. As we celebrate our 5th anniversary in El Salvador, we’ve seen how every new user, project, and innovation strengthens Bitcoin’s network. While El Salvador’s Bitcoin Law reforms are drawing attention, our focus remains on supporting adoption at every level. This year, we’re once again bringing together the builders, doers, and innovators — creating space for collaboration, conversation, and progress toward hyperbitcoinization.
